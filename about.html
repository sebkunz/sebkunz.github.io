Hi, my name is Sebastian and I am a Software Developer from Germany.

I am currently working for Daimler TSS GmbH, a Daimler AG subsidiary, in Stuttgart (Germany). My interests are mainly focused on backend development and operations, but I also like frontend development for mobile applications.

Born and raised in and around Stuttgart, my passion for cars, especially Mercedes Benz, developed early. After finishing my high school (german: Gymnasium), I started my Software Engineering studies at the University of Stuttgart.

During my studies, I got a student trainee at Daimler TSS and after finishing my Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering I joined Daimler TSS as a Software Developer. This was (and still is) a dream come true. Working for the inventors of the automobile.
