Hi, my name is Sebastian and I am a Software Developer from Germany.

I am currently working for Daimler TSS, a Daimler AG subsidiary, in Stuttgart (Germany) as Software Developer. Most of the time I am interested in backend development and operations, but some excursions into the frontend development are also welcome.

Born and raised in and around Stuttgart, my passion for cars, especially Mercedes Benz, developed early. After finishing my high school (german: Gymnasium), there was only one university to go: University of Stuttgart. The decision between Material Science and Software Engineering was a hard one, but at the end I decided to go with Software Engineering (which was the right decision).

During my studies, I got a student trainee at Daimler TSS and after finishing my Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering I joined Daimler TSS as a Software Developer. This was (and still is) a dream come true, working for the company I am so passionate about.
